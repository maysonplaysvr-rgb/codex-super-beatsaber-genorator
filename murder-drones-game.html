Copper 9 Survival

You are Uzi defending the bunker from incoming disassembly drones.

Clear waves, dodge attacks, and grab scrap to keep your score climbing.

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
Aim: Mouse
Shoot: Left Click
Restart: R

Click anywhere in the arena to start.

Tip: Yellow scrap heals a little. Survive long enough and the waves speed up.
